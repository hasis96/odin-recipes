Japanese Curry



Japanese curry is a comfort food that is usually eaten on a bed of rice but sometimes in the form of ramen!

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • A source of protein: Chicken/Beef/Pork
  • Japanese curry roux blocks
  • Chicken/Beef/Vegetable stock

Instructions

  1. Begin by slowly caramelizing your thinly sliced onion.
  2. While caramelizing the onions, cut the carrots and potatoes into bite-sized cubes.
  3. Once the onions are caramelized, add carrots and potatoes and fry for a few minutes.
  4. Pour enough stock to cover the vegetables
  5. Slice your choice of protein into cubes and drop into the pot
  6. Once vegetables are soft enough to pierce with a fork, and the protein is cooked, season with salt and pepper to taste!
  7. Serve over a bed of rice, or be creative and eat it with some noodles!
