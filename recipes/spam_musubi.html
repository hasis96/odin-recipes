Spam Musubi


Spam musubi is a piece of fried spam, covered in a sweet and savory soy sauce on rice and held together with seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Slice spam with desired thickness, then start frying until seared well
  2. Mix equal parts soy sauce and sugar, then pour into the pan when the spam is almost cooked
  3. Using the container of the spam (after washing), create a block of rice in the shape of the spam
  4. Stack the spam and rice, and use the seaweed to bind it all together!
